Comprehensive Cleaning Solutions

Prime Cleaning Services Ltd offers a wide range of cleaning services tailored to the specific needs of businesses and residential clients across London and the United Kingdom. Our services are delivered by trained professionals using approved products and methods.

Service Breakdown

  • Commercial and Office Cleaning

    Regular and deep cleaning for offices, retail spaces, and commercial premises. Includes dusting, vacuuming, sanitising surfaces, waste management, and restroom maintenance.

  • Residential Cleaning

    Scheduled and one-off cleaning for private homes and apartments. Services include kitchen and bathroom cleaning, floor care, window cleaning, and general tidying.

  • Carpet and Upholstery Cleaning

    Professional cleaning and stain removal for carpets, rugs, and upholstered furniture using safe and effective techniques.

  • End-of-Tenancy Cleaning

    Comprehensive cleaning for properties at the end of tenancy agreements, ensuring compliance with landlord and agency standards.

  • Specialist Sanitation and Disinfection

    Targeted cleaning and disinfection for high-risk environments, including healthcare facilities and food preparation areas, using certified products and procedures.

Service Scope and Exclusions

Our services cover routine and specialist cleaning tasks as outlined above. Exclusions include hazardous waste removal, pest control, and any services not explicitly listed in our service agreement.

Service Delivery

All services are provided under written agreements specifying the scope, frequency, and standards of work. Clients are responsible for providing access to premises and notifying us of any specific requirements or restrictions.

Quality Standards and Compliance

We maintain rigorous quality control procedures, including regular inspections and client feedback mechanisms. All cleaning products and methods comply with UK health and safety regulations.

Client Responsibilities

  • Provide timely access to premises
  • Communicate any special requirements in advance
  • Ensure payment of invoices as per agreed terms
  • Report any issues or concerns promptly
